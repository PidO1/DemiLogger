Welcome

This is the North-West Univarsity's lecturer assistant web-page. The amount of work to be done by lecturers in their area is sometimes near impossible for them to do everything they need to do, not to mention at the standards that are expected. In order to make these workloads easier, the North-West Univarsity has a lecturer assistant program that allow students to take part in this colossal undertaking. The lecturer assistant program is a program designed to allow students to help out in various tasks given by lecturers in hopes of not only lightening the work load of those lectures, but also give students the change to acquire much needed skills for later use in life. At the end students also get paid fair amounts of money to make all the hard work and effort of semesters worthwhile.

How to apply

At the beginning of each semester there are current modules that need student assistants. According to student requirements, they can apply for certain modules by filling in the student assistant form provided on the web-page that requests a set amount of information.
